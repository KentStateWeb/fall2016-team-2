Amenities

Located just 45 minutes northeast of Columbus and 80 minutes south of Cleveland, Buckeye Bed and Breakfast is the perfect location for a special occasion or weekend getaway. Our Lakeside cottage provides a cozy atmosphere with privacy and peaceful, serene surroundings for a relaxing getaway. Our spectacular setting offers breathtaking views of Lake Buckhorn and the surrounding scenic wooded areas. The grounds feature beautiful walking and hiking trails, a beautiful courtyard and garden, creeks, outdoor patios and scenic rooftop decks. Canoes and bikes are available on request along with maps of the grounds.
